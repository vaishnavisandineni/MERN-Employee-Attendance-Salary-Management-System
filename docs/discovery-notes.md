# Discovery Notes

## What does this system actually do end-to-end?
This HRMS system manages the complete lifecycle of construction workers—from onboarding and marking daily attendance to processing leave requests and ultimately generating their monthly salary. It replaces manual ledgers and ensures that daily wage earners are tracked properly across multiple construction sites.

## What is the final output of the system?
The final output is the **Salary/Payslip Calculation** generated at the end of the month based on the exact number of 'Present' days and approved 'Leave' days.

## Who depends on it most?
**Construction Workers (Daily wage earners).** Their livelihood depends directly on the accuracy of this system. Secondary dependents are Payroll Operators and Site Managers who use it to prevent manual calculation errors.

## What breaks in real life if it fails?
- **Financial Loss:** Workers might not get paid for days they worked, leading to strikes or severe personal financial distress.
- **Operational Halts:** If the admin dashboard crashes (e.g., due to deleted user bugs), payroll cannot be processed, delaying salaries for the entire workforce.
- **Overpayment Risk:** A bug allowing duplicate attendance marks could cost the company significant money through unwarranted payouts.
